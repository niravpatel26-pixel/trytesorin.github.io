Category clarity · Planning-first personal finance

Personal finance planning app — from first plan to long-term wealth.

Most apps help you track the past or push products. TESORIN helps you make the next right move — and it grows with you through a three-stage system: TESORIN → Fintrek → SummitVest.

Planning comes before budgeting and investing.

Budgeting tells you where money went. Investing tells you what money might earn. Planning tells you what to do next — given your real life, obligations, and goals.

Why budgeting apps fall short

Categories don’t automatically become decisions.

Many people stop logging after a few weeks. Even with perfect categories, they still don’t know how much flexibility they truly have.

Why investing apps aren’t enough

Investing is a tool. Planning is the system.

Investing assumes your foundation is stable. Planning makes sure you invest with confidence — without creating cash stress or fragile finances.

One philosophy. Three stages of depth.

Your needs evolve as income, assets, and responsibilities grow. TESORIN is designed so you don’t have to reset — you simply graduate into deeper tools when it becomes valuable.
