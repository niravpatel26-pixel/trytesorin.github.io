A planning-first money app designed for clarity, safety, and steady progress.

TESORIN is a calm place to plan your money. It’s built for people who want steady progress—without spreadsheets, noise, or a trading mindset.

Clarity
See your money in a simple view you can actually act on.
Safety first
Buffers and high-interest debt come before “optimizing.”
Steady progress
Small steps that compound—built for real life, not perfection.
No hype
No stock tips. No pressure. No “always be trading” energy.

Most money tools push you into extremes: complex spreadsheets you don’t keep up with, or trading-style apps that make you feel like you should be “on” all the time. TESORIN sits in the middle.

It’s for people thinking: “I earn okay money, but I don’t really have a plan.” Not stock tips. Not crypto bets. Just something solid you can follow.

The name is based on the idea of a treasure you build over time—something you shape deliberately, not something you gamble on.
  • Wealth as a path you walk, not a jackpot you wait for.
  • Planning that respects small starts and messy realities.
  • Tools that lower anxiety instead of raising your heart rate.

TESORIN is a planning surface for income, spending, buffers, and goals—built with safety before optimization.

It’s not a trading app. Not a course. Not a place that tries to scare you into action.

TESORIN is the first step. Over time, we plan to build deeper tools as money gets more complex:
  • TESORIN — the starting point: clarity, buffers, early wealth building.
  • FinTrek — deeper planning for scenarios and long-term detail.
  • SummitVest — premium tools for complex situations and larger wealth.
TL;DR
  • TESORIN is a calm planning app—not a trading screen.
  • Built for people taking money seriously for the first time.
  • Clarity first, safety first, then steady progress.
  • No stock tips. No spam. No pressure.
