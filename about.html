What this product is trying to be.

TESORIN is a calm place to plan your money. It’s built for people who want steady progress—without spreadsheets, noise, or a trading mindset.

A few things worth knowing

Open what you care about. Everything else can be skipped.

A lot of money tools pull you toward one of two extremes: complex tracking you don’t keep up with, or trading-style apps that keep you “on” all the time.

TESORIN is built for a different moment: “I earn okay money, but I don’t really have a plan.” Not stock tips. Not hype. Just a calmer way to decide what matters next—this month—without backtracking.

TESORIN comes from the idea of a treasure you build over time—not a lottery ticket. It reflects a simple belief: money planning should feel deliberate and steady, not like a gamble.
  • Small starts count.
  • Less anxiety is a feature.
  • Simple rules beat perfect spreadsheets.

Underneath the UI, TESORIN is a set of simple rules designed to keep you safe, focused, and moving— not to sell you something.
  • A planning surface for spending, buffers, and goals in one calm view.
  • Safety before optimization: buffers and high-interest debt first, then investing.
  • Plain language. Realistic inputs. No shame.
It’s not a trading app, not an investing course, and not a place that tries to make you feel behind.

TESORIN is designed to be the first step. Over time, it can sit alongside deeper tools as your money life becomes more complex:
  • TESORIN — calm planning for cashflow, buffers, and early wealth building.
  • FinTrek — planned as a deeper planning layer for more scenarios and long-term detail.
  • SummitVest — planned as premium tools and strategies for complex situations and larger wealth.
The feel stays the same: calm, transparent, and user-first.
TL;DR
  • TESORIN is a calm planning app, not a trading screen.
  • It’s for people taking money seriously for the first time—starting from small, realistic steps.
  • It focuses on safety and steady progress, before anything fancy.
  • No hidden product push. No commission-driven recommendations.
  • As your situation grows, the ecosystem can grow with you (FinTrek and SummitVest).
