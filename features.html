TESORIN FEATURES

Everything you need to see your money clearly.

TESORIN is not an investing course and not a trading app. It is a calm planning surface that shows your cash-flow, priorities, and future in one view — then nudges you towards better decisions, month after month.

The core of the product.

The first version of TESORIN focuses on three things: one honest snapshot, a safety-first plan, and gentle guidance you can follow even with small starts.

CLARITY

One clean wealth snapshot.

Pull together your income, spending, savings, and goals into a single, calm view. No spreadsheet tabs, no juggling five different apps — just one place that shows “where things stand” at a glance.

PRIORITIES

Safety before optimisation.

TESORIN automatically stacks your plan: first buffers and essentials, then high-interest debt, then long-term investing. You always know why money is going where it’s going — and what can wait.

GUIDANCE

Simple, monthly moves.

Instead of dashboards full of charts, you get a handful of suggested ranges: how much to keep as breathing room, how much to save, and how much can safely go toward future wealth each month.

What it feels like to use TESORIN.

The experience is deliberately quiet. TESORIN doesn’t shout at you with notifications or chase you into trades — it sits in the background, ready when you are.

Calm by default. Colours, typography, and layout are designed to be soft on the eye so you can think clearly about your money instead of reacting to flashing numbers.

Built for first-time planners. You never have to know the “right” financial jargon. Inputs use plain language, and explanations stay short and human.

Honest trade-offs. TESORIN shows what happens if you save more, save less, or shift priorities — without telling you there is only one correct path.

On the roadmap
Where TESORIN is heading next.

Early versions focus on planning only. Future phases will connect your plan to actual accounts through FINTREK and SUMMITVEST — for deeper planning and higher-touch wealth management — always with your interests first, never driven by commissions.
