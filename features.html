Features · Planning-first

A calm planning surface that tells you what to do next.

See your money clearly, understand what’s safe, and take one or two useful actions each month.

Your anchor number: monthly flexibility — what you can safely route to goals after essentials.

The core of the product.

Three pillars: clarity, priorities, and guidance — designed for small starts and steady progress.

CLARITY

One clean snapshot.

See income, essentials, goals, and balances in one place — without spreadsheet work or juggling multiple apps.

PRIORITIES

A plan in the right order.

Safety first, then high-interest debt, then long-term wealth. You always know what matters now — and what can wait.

GUIDANCE

Simple monthly actions.

Get clear ranges for what to keep as breathing room and what to route to goals — focused, actionable, and calm.

What it feels like to use.

Quiet by design — so the plan is easier to follow, not harder to maintain.

Quiet interface. Built to reduce anxiety and help you think clearly.

Plain language. Beginner-friendly without sounding childish.

Clear trade-offs. Adjust goals and see what changes — without “one perfect answer.”

On the roadmap
Planning first, always.

TESORIN starts as planning-only. Over time, deeper planning and premium strategy layers may exist — always transparent, always user-first.
