A calm planning surface that tells you what to do next.

See your money clearly, understand what’s safe, and take one or two useful actions each month — without product pushing or noisy dashboards.

Your anchor number: monthly flexibility — what you can safely route to goals after essentials.

The core of the product.

Three pillars: clarity, priorities, and guidance — designed for small starts and steady progress.

Clarity

One clean snapshot.

See income, essentials, goals, and balances in one place — without spreadsheet work.

Priorities

A plan in the right order.

Safety first, then high-interest debt, then long-term wealth — so you always know what matters now.

Guidance

Simple monthly actions.

Clear ranges for what to keep as breathing room and what to route to goals — focused and realistic.

What it feels like to use.

Quiet by design — so the plan is easier to follow, not harder to maintain.

Calm interface. Built to reduce anxiety and help you think clearly.

Plain language. Beginner-friendly without being oversimplified.

Clear trade-offs. Adjust goals and see what changes — without pretending there’s one perfect answer.
