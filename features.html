What's inside

Six tools. One coherent plan.

Not more charts. Not more categories. The minimum needed to make the next month clearer than the last.

Snapshot

A clean view of your baseline.

Income, fixed commitments, key bills, and balances — organised so you can reason about the month quickly.

Routing

A safe amount to allocate.

TESORIN separates what must stay from what can move — so you're not guessing what's okay to commit.

Priorities

A stable order of focus.

When trade-offs appear, the plan leans toward stability first. No re-deciding from scratch each month.

Goals

Targets you can sustain.

Set realistic targets and see what they imply for the rest of the month before you commit.

Adjustments

"What changes if…" checks.

Raise, rent change, family support — update one input and see downstream impact immediately.

Check-ins

Lightweight progress tracking.

On track, behind, or ahead — without turning your life into a logging exercise.

Design boundaries

Built to reduce decision load.

These are deliberate constraints — not gaps. TESORIN is a planning layer. It does not replace your bank, your broker, or your advisor.

Not thisInvestment recommendations or specific asset picks. Those stay with you or your broker.
Not thisSpending category tracking. TESORIN plans forward — it doesn't monitor the past.
Not thisReal-time account data or bank linking. You enter what matters. Nothing else is pulled.
Not thisTax or legal advice. TESORIN provides planning support — not professional advice of any kind.

Ready to see it in practice? The process takes about ten minutes.
