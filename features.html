What TESORIN helps you do.

These are the capabilities you’ll use month to month: keep a clean picture of your finances, translate it into a workable plan, and adjust without rebuilding everything.

Want the flow view? See how it works →

Core capabilities

A plan you can keep consistent.

Built around decisions you’ll actually make: what’s fixed, what’s flexible, what to focus on next, and what changes when life changes.

Snapshot

A clean view of your baseline.

Income, fixed commitments, key bills, and balances — organized so you can reason about your month quickly.

Routing

A safe amount to allocate.

TESORIN separates what must stay from what can move, so you’re not guessing what’s okay to commit.

Priorities

A stable order of focus.

When trade-offs show up, the plan leans toward stability first — so you don’t need to re-decide from scratch.

Goals

Goal targets you can sustain.

Set realistic targets and see what they imply for the rest of the month before you commit.

Adjustments

“What changes if…” checks.

Raise, rent change, travel, family support — update inputs and see the downstream impact immediately.

Check-ins

Lightweight progress tracking.

Simple check-ins for the plan itself: are you on track, behind, or ahead — without turning life into logging.

Design boundaries

Built to reduce decision load.

Not more charts. Not more categories. Just the minimum needed to make the next month clearer than the last.

What TESORIN emphasizes
  • Stable inputs over perfect accounting.
  • Trade-offs made explicit before you commit.
  • Plans that stay usable when life gets messy.
What TESORIN avoids
  • Busy dashboards designed to keep you watching.
  • Pressure to micromanage every purchase.
  • Product pushing disguised as “advice.”
