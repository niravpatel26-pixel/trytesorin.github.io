HOW TESORIN WORKS

From “I should plan” to “I have a plan”.

TESORIN stays very simple on the surface: a calm view of your money and a handful of clear next steps. Underneath, it uses rules that a good planner would — prioritising safety, debt, and long-term wealth in a way that fits your life.

Three calm steps

How TESORIN builds your plan with you.

In practice, using TESORIN feels like answering a few careful questions and getting back a path you can actually follow month by month.

You give TESORIN a lightweight picture of your current money:
  • Monthly income (after tax, roughly is fine).
  • Core spending and fixed obligations.
  • Any savings, debt, or investments you already have.
No spreadsheets, no bank-connection required at the start. Just enough detail to be honest with yourself.

Instead of asking “how much money do you want?”, TESORIN helps you set a few grounded milestones:
  • A safety buffer or emergency fund that lets you breathe.
  • Debt comfort targets (what you want to pay off, and by when).
  • Long-term “freedom” numbers — for future choices, not just retirement.
You choose what matters; TESORIN organises it into a simple priority stack.

TESORIN translates everything into amounts you can act on:
  • How much to keep as “breathing room” each month.
  • How much to direct to safety, debt, and longer-term investing.
  • How your numbers might evolve over the next few years.
The output is not a trading screen — it’s a plan you can actually stick to, even with small starts.
In plain language:

TESORIN behaves like a friendly planner who isn’t trying to sell you anything. It asks a few careful questions, shows you one clear picture, and then keeps nudging you towards better decisions — at your pace.

What you bring

A rough but honest snapshot of your money, a sense of what “secure” would feel like, and willingness to start small.

What TESORIN brings

A simple rules-based engine that prioritises safety, clarifies trade-offs, and keeps your attention on the next two or three moves — not market noise.

What you leave with

A serious, calm money plan you understand: how today’s choices support the life you’re trying to build over the next 5–10 years.

Under the hood

What TESORIN quietly does in the background.

On screen, you see simple numbers and ranges. Behind the scenes, TESORIN uses a few core principles to keep your plan sane and realistic — starting with India and Canada, and expanding from there.

TESORIN looks at how much actually stays in your hands each month, then suggests a buffer so surprise expenses don’t blow up your plan. No “optimize every rupee/dollar” pressure — just enough structure.

Before chasing returns, TESORIN nudges you to cover basics: emergency cash, high-interest debt, and near-term commitments. Only then does it point you toward longer-term investing conversations.

Future phases will connect your plan to products through FINTREK and SUMMITVEST. The design principle stays the same: your interests first, no hidden commissions, no “product hunt” inside the app.
