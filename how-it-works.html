How it works · Calm monthly planning

A calm way to plan money — one month at a time.

TESORIN turns your income and goals into a simple plan with clear monthly actions — without spreadsheets, pressure, or market noise.

Start without linking a bank. Add a simple snapshot and build from there.

The flow

The same calm loop, repeated.

You see what’s safe, choose a small action, and update when life changes.

Add just enough to be honest:
  • Income and fixed commitments.
  • Core spending and bills.
  • Savings, debt, and existing investments.
Manual entry works. Bank linking is optional.

TESORIN gives you one anchor number:
  • Monthly flexibility — what you can safely route to goals after essentials.
It’s meant to be usable, not perfect.

Your money gets organized into a sensible sequence:
  • Safety (buffers and breathing room).
  • High-interest debt.
  • Long-term wealth.
The goal is to reduce regret and keep the plan stable.

TESORIN keeps actions small and clear:
  • 1–2 moves that fit your flexibility number.
  • A “why” that explains impact.
  • Progress that feels steady, not dramatic.

Raise, rent change, travel, family support — the plan isn’t brittle. Update your snapshot and TESORIN recalculates what’s safe and what’s next.
What you’ll actually see
Monthly flexibility

One number that tells you what’s safe to allocate this month.

A priority stack

Safety → debt → wealth, in a calm sequence that stays realistic.

Clear actions

Small moves you can follow without turning life into tracking.

What it is not

Not a trading screen. Not stock tips. Not product pushing.

Under the hood

Conservative rules, chosen on purpose.

The interface is simple, but the system is designed to keep decisions stable and beginner-safe.

The plan starts from what reliably stays in your hands each month, not from idealized budgets.

Before chasing returns, TESORIN nudges buffers and expensive debt so the plan doesn’t break under stress.

Start simple. Add detail only when it helps. The goal is clarity, not complexity.
