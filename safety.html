Safety & trust · How TESORIN thinks about your money

How TESORIN thinks about safety.

TESORIN is being built to feel like a calm planning surface — not a sales funnel, not a casino, and not a place where your money is moved around without you noticing.

Important: nothing on TESORIN is personal financial, legal, or tax advice. It’s planning support and education to help you make your own decisions more clearly.

Safety pillars

How TESORIN stays in a safe lane.

Instead of one long policy wall, you can open specific sections about what TESORIN is, how it handles money, how it treats data, and what it cannot promise.

Clear boundaries are part of safety. TESORIN stays in the lane of planning and clarity, not product pushing or high-pressure sales.

TESORIN is:
  • A planning app that helps you see income, spending, buffers, and goals in one calm view.
  • A way to think through “what should I do next?” with your money, month by month.
  • Beginner-friendly: built for people taking money seriously for the first time.
TESORIN is not:
  • Not a bank or brokerage account.
  • Not a place that holds your money or executes trades on your behalf.
  • Not personal financial, legal, tax, or investment advice tailored to your full situation.
  • Not built around commissions for selling you specific products.
Planning, not trading Clarity first, products later Beginner-safe defaults

TESORIN starts from a conservative place: your actual money stays with your existing banks and investment platforms. TESORIN helps you plan around those accounts, not replace them.
  • No custody of your funds.
    TESORIN does not hold your money. Your cash and investments remain with your bank, broker, or other providers you already use.
  • No “hidden” transactions.
    TESORIN does not move money for you in the background. Any transfers or investments you make are done through your own bank or broker.
  • Future integrations will be cautious.
    When TESORIN eventually connects with third-party financial services, the goal is to work only with regulated providers and to explain clearly what each integration does.
  • You stay in control.
    You choose which accounts to connect (if any), what to act on, and when. TESORIN’s role is to make the choices clearer, not to decide for you.

Good planning requires honest numbers. That only works if you can trust how your information is handled.
  • Collect only what’s needed.
    TESORIN is being designed to work with simple, high-level inputs: income ranges, rough spending, debts, and goals — not endless forms or unnecessary personal details.
  • Respect by default.
    The intention is to use secure channels (like HTTPS) and reputable infrastructure to store data, with access limited to what’s required to run the product.
  • No silent selling of identifiable data.
    TESORIN is not being built around selling personally identifiable financial data to third parties.
  • Clear policies as the product matures.
    As TESORIN gets closer to launch, you’ll see a plain-language privacy policy and terms of use that explain what happens with your data in detail — no dark patterns, no buried surprises.

Honest tools need honest disclaimers. These are limits you should keep in mind while using TESORIN.
  • Not financial advice.
    TESORIN gives general planning frameworks and educational guidance. It does not know your entire situation, your risk tolerance, or your legal/tax constraints.
  • No guarantees.
    All projections, charts, and scenarios are estimates, not promises. Markets, laws, and your own life can change in ways nobody can fully predict.
  • You remain responsible for decisions.
    TESORIN can help you think more clearly, but decisions are always yours. When in doubt, speaking with a qualified human professional in your region is important.
  • Not a substitute for regulation.
    TESORIN is a planning layer on top of existing regulated financial systems. It is not itself a bank, broker, or regulated investment firm.

A big part of safety is knowing what not to trust. A few quick rules:
  • Check the URL.
    Official TESORIN pages will use the trytesorin.com domain (or clearly announced domains).
  • Be careful with links.
    Don’t log in or share sensitive information from links that look suspicious or come from people you don’t know.
  • Never share one-time codes.
    No one legitimate needs your bank OTP or full card details to “help” you use a planning app.
  • Ask questions.
    If any message or feature that claims to be from TESORIN doesn’t match what you see on the official site, treat it with caution and verify first.
