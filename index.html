How it works

From “I should plan” to “I have a plan”.

Tesorin sits where a good financial planner would — but on your phone, without the pressure, and built for beginners who want to get serious.

1 Tell us where you are today

A quick, honest picture

Add your income, rough spending, and what you’ve already saved. We keep it light, so you don’t get stuck in forms.

2 Choose what “wealth” means to you

Define your version of “ahead”

Pick milestones like a buffer fund, a freedom number, or a long-term net worth target. Tesorin doesn’t assume one life path.

Questions

Is Tesorin right for me?

Tesorin is for people who are done “winging it” with money, but don’t want to become full-time finance nerds. If you want a simple, serious starting point, you’re in the right place.

Do I need a lot of money to start?

No. Tesorin is deliberately built for small, realistic starting points. The question is not “how much do you have?” — it’s “what can we build from here?”.

Is this an investing or trading app?

No. Tesorin focuses on planning: your cash flow, buffers, and long-term wealth path. Any investing comes later, as an extension of a solid plan.
